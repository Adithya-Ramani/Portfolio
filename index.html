About Me

I’m Adithya, a Chartered Accountant working at the intersection of finance, automation, and product building. My focus is on rethinking accounting, reporting, reconciliation, and revenue recognition using better systems, integrations, and intelligence.

I believe businesses shouldn’t spend more than 5% of their time or P&L on accounting—and my work is centred on achieving reliable financial outcomes well within that boundary. From Deloitte’s Statutory Audit practice to working in the founder’s office of a legacy manufacturing business, I’ve had a front-row seat to how finance functions actually operate and where they break.

Through my firm, Kogentzar, I’ve partnered with platforms like Zoho, Tally, and Razorpay to implement systems and build low-code automations for SMBs. I’m deeply interested in SaaS and functional technology, and I believe finance can be made quieter, faster, and far less painful with the right design choices.
